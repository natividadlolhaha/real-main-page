Welcome to Tanay Tambay Treats!

A dedicated website to delicious recipes from Tanay!

Our website is all about documenting the unique and delicious recipes from all around Tanay. If you have been to Tanay before, you would know that the food that comes from Tanay is undeniably delicious as it's a rather popular place for motorcyclists to tambay and have food while doing so. So why not share these delicious delicacies with the rest of us at the comfort of our own homes! Join us as we document all the unique recipies that people have come up with!
